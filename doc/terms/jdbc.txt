The namespace <http://d2rq.org/terms/jdbc/> is used in D2RQ [1] to indicate
JDBC connection properties. See the D2RQ language specification [2] for more
information.

Currently, no machine-readable version of this namespace is available.


[1] http://d2rq.org/
[2] http://d2rq.org/d2rq-language#jdbc-connection-properties
